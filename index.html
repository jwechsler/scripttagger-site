ScriptTagger

Line notes at the speed of performance — for iPad and Mac.

What it does

ScriptTagger is for stage managers, assistant directors, and anyone on book during a run. Open your script PDF, and when an actor drops a line, paraphrases, or jumps a cue, capture it in a couple of taps — dropped, added, paraphrase, late, early, or off book. Every note records the exact script text, page, character, and time.

When the run ends, hand out reports that actors actually read: notes grouped by character, in show order, with surrounding context. Print them, save per-character PDFs, or share anywhere.

The app works entirely offline. No account, no cloud, no tracking — your script stays yours.
