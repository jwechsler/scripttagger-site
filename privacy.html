Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

ScriptTagger is developed by Theater Wit NFP (“we,” “us”). This policy describes how ScriptTagger for iPad and Mac handles your information.

The short version

ScriptTagger does not collect, transmit, store remotely, sell, or share any personal data. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

What the app stores, and where

  • Scripts (PDFs): Script files you open are read from locations you choose. The app keeps its working data — script metadata and your line notes — in a local database on your device only.
  • Line notes: Character names, note types, selected text, and timestamps you create are stored in that same local database. They never leave your device unless you explicitly export, print, or share them yourself.

What the app does not do

  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.
  • No user accounts, sign-in, or identifiers.
  • No network connections — the app makes no requests to the internet.
  • No access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, or photos.

Sharing and export

When you use Print, Save to Files, or Share, the resulting report goes only where you send it (your printer, your Files location, or the app you share to). We never receive a copy.

Data deletion

Deleting a script in the app deletes its notes. Deleting the app deletes all of the app's data from your device.

Children's privacy

ScriptTagger does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app changes any of the above (for example, by adding an optional sync feature), we will update this policy before that version is released and note the change here.
